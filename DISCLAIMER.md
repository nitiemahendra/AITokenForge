### Cost Estimates

Cost estimates provided by TokenForge are calculated using publicly available pricing data from AI model providers. These estimates:

- May not reflect the most current pricing (providers change prices without notice)
- Are approximations based on standard token counting heuristics
- Do not account for volume discounts, enterprise pricing, or special agreements
- Should not be used as the sole basis for financial decisions

Always verify costs against your actual provider billing.

### Optimization Results

Semantic similarity scores are computed using the `all-MiniLM-L6-v2` embedding model. These scores:

- Are a heuristic measure, not a guarantee of meaning preservation
- May not capture all nuances of domain-specific language
- Should be validated for critical use cases (legal, medical, compliance)

**Do not use Aggressive optimization mode for prompts where exact wording is legally or contractually significant.**
